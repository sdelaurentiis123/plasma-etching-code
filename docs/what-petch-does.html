What petch does — one page

The whole project in one screen: what it is, what's validated, against what, and what isn't.

petch is a 3-D feature-scale plasma-etch simulator: level-set surface evolution + flux transport + SF6/O2 surface chemistry, with the flux and level-set kernels in NVIDIA Warp — GPU-resident end-to-end (~1 s per etch, CPU-independent; ~8–15× faster than ViennaPS, whose level set is CPU-bound), differentiable by substrate, with a ViennaPS-shaped API: petch.Process(Domain.trench(...), SF6O2(), duration).run().

The three validated headlines

  1. It predicts a real wafer's deep tail it was never fitted to. Evolving petch, one transport knob calibrated on the de Boer knee (AR 10/20) — the held-out AR 40 point comes out 0.15–0.20 vs the measured 0.20 (full-curve RMSE 0.031–0.043, gate 0.05). Ballistic codes, ViennaPS included, structurally miss this. Experiments.
  2. Its charging solver reproduces the community reference with zero tuned constants. Floor ion flux vs AR: RMSE 0.039 vs Hwang–Giapis 1997; 300 eV ions correctly stay open at AR 4 (Matsui). ViennaPS has no charging model. Experiments.
  3. Its deterministic transport matches a measured ViennaPS reference (radiosity+GMRES, RMSE 0.043) — and the numerics agreement comes with a like-for-like speed result: ~8–15× (CPU-dependent; petch ~1 s, GPU-resident). ViennaPS Validation, Performance.
Honest limits

One wafer dataset, one chemistry (de Boer cryo SF6/O2; the Gomez yield gate and a second dataset are next). Two calibrated constants: the Knudsen floor-loss knob (fitted on the knee; the tail is then predicted) and one absolute-rate constant (1.3 µm/min, consistent across features to 1.1%) — everything else is cited. DDA quarantined in the mirror-wall regime. Charging: insulating floors only (v1 table at Hwang–Giapis conditions); its foot-energy gate currently fails (V-floor offset, RF-burst physics missing) so the wired path uses the published deflected-energy table; notch-depth validation vs Nozawa/Fujiwara awaits multi-material etch-stop. Every number above has a reproducer script and a pass/fail gate in the repo (RECONCILIATION.md is the full log, retractions included).
